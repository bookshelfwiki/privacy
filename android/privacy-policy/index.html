Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the bookshelfwiki Android application ("the App") handles information in relation to users.

By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection by the App

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data or personally identifiable information from users, except for limited technical information required to ensure app functionality, stability, and updates (see Sections 6 and 7).

Specifically, the App:

  • does not require user registration
  • does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • does not collect documents, images, or scanned content
  • does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

All core functionality, including document scanning, is performed locally on the user's device.

3. Device Permissions

The App may request access to the device camera in order to enable document scanning functionality.

All images captured using the camera are processed locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to external servers by the App.

The App does not access the camera in the background and only uses it when the user actively initiates scanning.

4. On-Device Processing

The App uses on-device machine learning capabilities provided by Google ML Kit. All image processing and document detection occur locally on the device.

No user content processed by these features is transmitted to external servers by the App.

5. Third-Party Services and System Dependencies

The App relies on system-level services provided by Google, including Google Play Services ML Kit.

While the App itself does not collect personal data, these services may collect limited technical information (such as device information, diagnostics, and usage data) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and applicable service documentation.

6. Crash Reports and Diagnostics

To improve app stability and fix bugs, the App may collect and transmit limited technical diagnostic information in the event of an error.

This may include:

  • stack traces
  • device type and operating system version
  • app version
  • technical information about the error

This information:

  • is used solely for debugging and improving the App
  • is not used to identify users
  • is not combined with other data to create user profiles

The App does not intentionally collect any documents, images, or user-generated content as part of crash reporting.

7. Over-the-Air Updates

The App may use over-the-air update technology to deliver improvements and bug fixes without requiring a full app store update.

To check for and download updates, the App may contact update servers (such as those provided by Expo or a self-hosted equivalent). As part of this process, limited technical information may be transmitted, including:

  • device and operating system information
  • app version
  • IP address

This information is used solely to deliver the appropriate update and ensure compatibility. It is not used to identify users.

8. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Any limited technical data collected for diagnostics or updates is used solely for maintaining and improving the App.

9. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
